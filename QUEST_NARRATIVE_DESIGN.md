## üé≠ THE OVERARCHING NARRATIVE

### The Two Intertwined Threats

**THREAT 1: The Shadow Rising (Malachar)**
The ancient wizard Malachar, thought dead for centuries, stirs in the Shadow Tower. His dark magic seeps across the land, corrupting creatures, causing strange phenomena, and preparing for his return.

**THREAT 2: The Merchant Conspiracy (The Black Ledger)**
A secret cabal of corrupt merchants called "The Black Ledger" has been funding Malachar's return, believing they can control him and monopolize all trade. They're wrong, but their greed blinds them.

**THE TWIST:** These threats are intertwined. The Black Ledger funds Malachar. Defeating one weakens the other. The player discovers this connection in Act 3.

## üèõÔ∏è THE 5-ACT STRUCTURE

### ACT 1: "A Trader's Beginning" (Quests 1-7)
**Theme:** Establish yourself, learn the world
**Wealth Gate:** None (starting act)
**Regions:** Greendale (starter), Sunhaven (south)
**Main Threat Revealed:** Strange rumors, minor disturbances

### ACT 2: "Whispers of Conspiracy" (Quests 8-14)
**Theme:** Discover something is wrong, investigate
**Wealth Gate:** TRADER rank (5,000g) - scaled by difficulty
**Regions:** Ironforge, Jade Harbor, Eastern territories
**Main Threat Revealed:** The Black Ledger exists, funding something dark

### ACT 3: "The Dark Connection" (Quests 15-21)
**Theme:** Link Malachar and Black Ledger, choose sides
**Wealth Gate:** MAGNATE rank (50,000g) - scaled by difficulty
**Regions:** Royal Capital, Northern territories
**Main Threat Revealed:** Malachar is real, Black Ledger controls supply chains

### ACT 4: "War of Commerce" (Quests 22-28)
**Theme:** Economic warfare, sabotage, alliance building
**Wealth Gate:** TYCOON rank (150,000g) - scaled by difficulty
**Regions:** Western territories, Frostholm
**Main Threat Revealed:** Final preparations for assault

### ACT 5: "The Final Reckoning" (Quests 29-35)
**Theme:** Assault both strongholds, end the threats
**Wealth Gate:** BARON rank (500,000g) - scaled by difficulty
**Regions:** Shadow Tower, Ruins of Malachar
**Main Threat Revealed:** Portal to Doom World opens

## üåã DOOM WORLD SYSTEM

### Concept
When player defeats EITHER main dungeon boss (Shadow Tower Guardian or Ruins Guardian), a portal opens. Player can choose to enter the DOOM WORLD - an apocalyptic alternate reality where Malachar won and the world fell to darkness.

### Portal Mechanics
- **Two dungeons** (Shadow Tower in west, Ruins of Malachar in north) each have a portal
- **Defeating a boss** opens that portal permanently (until boss respawns)
- **Enter via boss victory panel** - shows loot, stats, and "Enter Doom World" button
- **Exit only via dungeon safe zones** - the dungeon you entered from is safe
- **Other dungeon portal** is also safe zone - must travel through Doom World to reach it
- **Tracked quests still visible** but tooltip warns "Escape the Doom World first!"

### Doom World Map
Uses existing doom map (debooger "doom" command). Same location positions but:
- All locations renamed (Royal Capital ‚Üí Destroyed Royal Capital)
- Inns ‚Üí Inn Wreckage
- Markets ‚Üí Ruined Market (still function with doom economy)
- Same NPCs but with doom-specific dialogue and API instructions

### Doom World Economy

**Price Multipliers:**
| Item Type | Normal World | Doom World |
|-----------|--------------|------------|
| Food | 1.0x | 10x |
| Water | 1.0x | 15x |
| Medicine | 1.0x | 12x |
| Weapons | 1.0x | 3x |
| Luxury goods | 1.0x | 0.1x |
| Gold value | Full | 0.3x (barter preferred) |

**Barter System:**
- Gold has 30% normal purchasing power
- Direct item-for-item trades preferred
- Survival items are the true currency

### Doom World Locations (Corrupted versions)
- Doom Greendale (burned ruins)
- Doom Sunhaven (flooded, monsters)
- Doom Ironforge (enslaved forges)
- Doom Capital (throne of darkness)
- Doom Frostholm (eternal blizzard)
- Doom Western (shadow wasteland)

### Doom World NPCs
- Survivors (desperate, trade survival goods)

### Doom World Bosses

### Doom World Central Boss: GREEDY WON

**Location:** Destroyed Royal Capital (center of doom map)
**Lore:** The merchant conspiracy won in this timeline. "Greedy Won" is the twisted manifestation of unchecked greed - what the Black Ledger became when they got everything they wanted. A grotesque fusion of gold, contracts, and suffering.

**Boss Stats:**
- Health: 1000
- Damage: 30-50
- Defense: 25
- Special Attacks:
  - "Golden Grasp" - steals player gold during fight
  - "Contract Curse" - applies damage over time
  - "Market Crash" - reduces player's max health temporarily

**Rewards:**
- Title: "Doom Ender"
- Armor: "Greed's End" set (doom-only legendary)
- Gold: 25,000 (doom currency - worth less in normal world)
- Unlocks: Fast travel between all doom locations
- Achievement: "Greed Defeated"

### Doom Markets
Every location that has a market in normal world has a "Ruined Market" in Doom World:
- Same merchant NPCs but doom-altered personalities
- Doom economy prices (survival items expensive, luxury cheap)
- Player can buy/sell and carry goods between worlds for profit
- Arbitrage opportunity: buy cheap luxury in Doom, sell high in Normal

### Doom World Exclusive Items
- Doom Rations (best food, Doom-only)
- Shadow Steel (crafting material)
- Essence of Despair (alchemy)
- Survivor's Journal (lore)
- Blighted equipment set
